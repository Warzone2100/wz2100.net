With the help of many bug reporters & contributors, we're happy to announce the release of **4.6.3**, a bug-fix and improvement release.

Including:
- **Terrain shadows**
  - Terrain can now cast actual shadows on itself and everything else
  - Configurable in Graphics options
- **Numerous improvements for shadows, lighting, and fog**
- Cobra AI: Improve cyborg style games
- Nexus AI: Send help chat messages to allies only
- Fix: Video sequence skip time delay for powerful systems
- Fix: VTOLs floating motionless in mid-air after failed embark
- Fix: Auto-embark transporter selection behavior in skirmish / mp
- Fix: Black borders when changing display scale while fullscreen on some platforms
- Fix: Auto-not-ready kick behavior in multiplayer
- Fix: Improve accuracy of multiStatsResearchPerformance accumulation
- Fix: Various possible causes of rare desyncs in multiplayer
- Numerous fixes for crashes, cleanup issues, and memory leaks

[View the full 4.6.3 changelog](https://github.com/Warzone2100/warzone2100/raw/4.6.3/ChangeLog)
